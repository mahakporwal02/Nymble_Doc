synchronized Bitmap som.instruction.request.visionCamera.camera.CameraV8_3.getStillCapture ( ) throws InterruptedException

Uses the CameraExecutor#captureImage() to capture an image. It waits on a countdown latch shared with the bitmap listener for atmost 10 seconds. And then returns. If the timeout occurred, then the visionImage will be null.
