void com.example.recipeengine.engine.chef.BaseChef.startCooking ( )

EXECUTES A SINGLE INSTRUCTION AT A TIME Gets an instruction from the BaseChef#instructionProvider. Gets the executor for the instruction from the BaseChef#executorFactory.
